About Me

I’m a software engineer with a nontraditional background, transitioning into tech after serving in the U.S. Navy and working in healthcare. Those experiences shaped how I approach problem-solving: methodically, with attention to detail, and a strong focus on reliability and accountability.

I’m completing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and building a portfolio of projects in C#, ASP.NET Core, and cloud-based application development. My work emphasizes clear architecture, validated inputs, and readable, maintainable code.

I enjoy building practical applications that simplify complex processes or help users make informed decisions, and I value communication and execution that makes software easy to explain to both technical and non-technical audiences.
